Lets get Personal

Are you ready to make a shift? Its time to dream big.

When life breaks you, it’s because you are ready to be put back together differently. Every piece of you that feels shattered, is a piece that will find a new place, a new purpose, a new meaning. Trust that the cracks are where the light gets in.

Hey there, I'm Tricia.

A certified Conscious Uncoupling and Relationship Coach, trained and personally mentored by Katherine Woodward Thomas, the New York Times bestselling author of Conscious Uncoupling: 5 Steps to Live Happily Even After.

My mission is to uplift and inspire women as they navigate the often-devastating process of ending a significant relationship. Having been down this challenging path myself, I'm here to help you channel your heartbreak into a powerful opportunity for growth, supporting you in creating a more fulfilling life.

From Long Island, New York, I work globally, connecting with clients from all walks of life who are ready to rebuild with intention and hope

Where I started

Making Food The Language of Love

Before becoming a coach, I spent years as a professional chef, dedicated to making beautiful food and to creating warmth and connection around the table.My Italian parents and their culinary heritage contributed significantly to my love of all things having to do with food. Today, I bring that same sense of warmth and nourishment into my coaching, fostering an environment where my clients feel seen, supported, and encouraged to reach for a better, more fulfilling life. Just as a recipe can be transformed with care and the right ingredients, I believe that every woman has the potential to transform her life after loss and to rediscover her own passions, joy, and purpose.

My 5-Step Journey to Freedom and Personal Power

My approach combines a powerful fusion of intuitive and spiritual guidance with a proven, strategic 5-Step plan that can enable you to reclaim your purpose and to create a life bigger and better than the one you are trying to leave behind. I’ve been where you are, on the edge of a breakthrough, yet confused and unsure of my next steps. But when I was introduced to the Conscious Uncoupling process, I took a leap of faith, transitioned out of an unhappy marriage and stepped into my own personal power. That’s where and when my transformation began. Today I’m living my best life! I now live an expanded new life in a beautiful home where I love entertaining friends and getting to know new ones too. I am blessed to be able to travel often to interesting places around the world, soaking up different cultures and learning about their cuisines. I have successfully redefined my relationship with my former partner and am slowly building the foundation for a deep and lasting friendship. Mostly, I feel blessed for my own profound personal transformation and for the opportunity to restart my life and create the next chapter. Best of all, as a Conscious Uncoupling and Relationship Coach, I have the honor of doing this joyful, hopeful work that allows me to help to heal heartbreak at its core. Trust me, you can make this shift in your life too!
